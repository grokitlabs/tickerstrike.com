// FAQ

Questions, answered

What is TickerStrike?

A stock research management platform that ingests every source you trust, synthesizes it into a living thesis per ticker, and verifies the edge behind your setups — so you can determine when and where is the right place to strike using a targeted plan with surgical precision.

What sources can it ingest?

Email newsletters (Substack and others), X accounts and ticker searches, Patreon research, PDFs, arbitrary websites, stock price data, and options data including unusual flow. If it informs your trades, the goal is to get it on the ticker's evidence stream.

Is this a signal service?

No. The theses are built from your sources and the edges are your setups, verified. TickerStrike organizes and stress-tests your research — it doesn't sell you picks.

Is TickerStrike a broker? Does it place trades?

No. TickerStrike is a research and analysis tool. It tells you when your case is strongest — execution happens at your broker of choice.

Is this investment advice?

No. TickerStrike synthesizes and tests your own research. Nothing on the platform constitutes investment advice, and trading involves risk.
