// FAQ

Questions, answered

What is TickerStrike?

A stock research management platform. It helps you build investment theses, collect supporting data from disparate sources, synthesize it, and optionally backtest strategies — so you can enter trades at the right moment with the highest strike rate possible.

Is TickerStrike a broker? Does it place trades?

No. TickerStrike is a research and analysis tool. It helps you decide when to strike — execution happens at your broker of choice.

Do I need to use the backtesting features?

No, backtesting is optional. Many users start with thesis building and data collection, then layer in backtesting to quantify their edge.

Is this investment advice?

No. TickerStrike organizes your own research and analysis. Nothing on the platform constitutes investment advice, and trading involves risk.
